2. Before You Email

To help us resolve your issue faster, please include:

  1. App version (App Store / TestFlight version or Settings screen)
  2. Device model and iOS or Android version
  3. ChronicOS plan (Free, Premium, or Family) if relevant
  4. Steps to reproduce the problem
  5. Screenshots if helpful — please redact personal health information
  6. Apple ID email (for subscription issues only; never send passwords)

Support is provided on a commercially reasonable basis by an individual developer. We do not guarantee a specific resolution time or outcome for every request.

3. Frequently Asked Questions

Getting started & onboarding

  • First launch: Welcome screen → select conditions → privacy consent → main app.
  • Replay welcome guide: Profile → Settings & privacy → Replay welcome guide. Does not delete your health data.
  • Demo data: On the welcome screen, View Demo loads sample data for exploration.

Free vs Premium vs Family

Feature area Free Premium / Family
Symptoms, meds, timeline, rule alerts, interaction scan, visit records, between-visit coach Included Included
Active conditions & doctors Limited (see in-app) Unlimited
AI appointment prep, provider summaries, lab AI, voice structuring Uses monthly Health AI Points Higher monthly point allowance
Caregiver collaboration, family dashboard, multiple profiles — Family plan

Subscriptions & Health AI Points

  • Premium (~$14.99/month): ~200 Health AI Points/month — Product ID com.jchen.ChronicOS.premium.monthly
  • Family (~$29.99/month): ~500 points/month + caregiver features — com.jchen.ChronicOS.family.monthly
  • AI Boost Packs (one-time): 50 points (boost.50) or 100 points (boost.100)
  • Manage or cancel (iOS): Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → ChronicOS
  • Refunds: Request through Apple or Google first; we cannot override store refund decisions
  • Points not working after purchase: Force-quit and reopen the app; restore purchases if available; email us with your store receipt

Export, delete, and privacy

  • Export data (GDPR): Profile → Settings & privacy → Export my data (JSON)
  • Delete all data: Profile → Settings & privacy → Delete all data — permanent on that device
  • Privacy Policy: privacy-policy.html
  • Terms of Use: eula.html

Account & cloud sync

  • Sign-in is optional for local use; required for cloud backup/sync when enabled
  • Sync errors such as MISSING_API_BASE_URL, NOT_SIGNED_IN, or network failures — check internet connection and sign-in status
  • FHIR / patient portal: Profile → connect flow; requires valid portal credentials

Caregivers

  • Profile → Caregiver → invite or accept with token
  • Permission levels control diary, medications (read-only), and visit access
  • You are responsible for who you invite to view health information

Medications & reminders

  • Add meds under the Meds tab; interaction scan runs locally on changes
  • Enable reminders on each medication; allow notifications when iOS prompts you
  • Side-effect diary and clinician export PDF available from medication detail

Labs, coach & visit prep

  • Labs: View trends; AI deeper explanation uses Health AI Points (1 pt)
  • Between-visit coach: Profile → Coach — weekly goals by disease combination (free)
  • Visit prep: Visit tab; AI prep uses points; PDF export available on Premium

Notifications not working

  • iOS: Settings → ChronicOS → Notifications → Allow Notifications
  • Ensure medication reminders are enabled on each med entry
  • Background refresh and Do Not Disturb can delay alerts

App crashes or won’t open

  • Update to the latest App Store version
  • Restart your device
  • If persistent, email us with device model, OS version, and what you were doing before the crash

4. Billing & Subscription Support

All payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. For charges, renewals, or refunds:

  • Apple: reportaproblem.apple.com or Apple Support
  • Google: Google Play → Payments & subscriptions

Email us at hello@familymedvault.com for help understanding your ChronicOS plan or point balance after you have checked your store subscription status.

5. Accessibility & Language

ChronicOS supports English and Chinese (中文) in-app. Toggle language from the welcome screen or Profile → Settings. If you encounter accessibility barriers, describe them in your support email and we will consider improvements in future updates.

6. Security Reports

To report a security vulnerability, email hello@familymedvault.com with subject ChronicOS Security Report. Please do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities before we have had a reasonable time to respond.

7. Support Limitations

Technical support covers app installation, account, subscriptions, data export/deletion, and feature questions. We do not provide:

  • Medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations
  • Emergency response or on-call clinical services
  • Guaranteed data recovery after device loss or uninstall without cloud backup
  • Support for modified, jailbroken, or unsupported devices beyond commercially reasonable efforts

ChronicOS is operated by an individual developer. Support is best-effort and subject to the limitations of liability in our Terms of Use.
