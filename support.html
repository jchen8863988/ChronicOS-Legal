3. About ChronicOS

ChronicOS helps adults organize and track multiple chronic conditions, medications, symptoms, labs, appointments, and caregiver collaboration. The App uses five main tabs:

  • Home — condition dashboard, trend charts with clinical reference bands, alert center, Health Coach entry
  • Timeline — unified symptom, voice, and event history
  • Doctors — provider list and visit preparation
  • Medications — medication list, safety scanning, reminders, side-effect diary
  • Profile — account, settings, Health Coach, caregiver tools, family dashboard, legal links

ChronicOS is not a medical device, healthcare provider, insurer, or emergency service. Alerts, coaching tips, chart reference lines, and AI outputs are informational only and are not medical advice.

Subscriptions & Health AI Points

  • Premium (~$14.99/month, auto-renewable): ~200 Health AI Points/month — Product ID com.jchen.ChronicOS.premium.monthly
  • Family (~$29.99/month, auto-renewable): ~500 points/month + family & caregiver features — com.jchen.ChronicOS.family.monthly
  • AI Boost Packs (one-time consumables): 50 points (com.jchen.ChronicOS.boost.50, ~$4.99) or 100 points (com.jchen.ChronicOS.boost.100, ~$7.99)
  • What points are used for: AI appointment prep, provider summaries, lab interpretation, voice structuring, longitudinal analysis, medication interaction explanations, and similar optional AI features
  • Manage or cancel (iOS): Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → ChronicOS
  • Manage or cancel (Android): Google Play → Payments & subscriptions
  • Refunds: Request through Apple or Google first; we cannot override store refund decisions
  • Points not appearing after purchase: Force-quit and reopen the app; use Restore Purchases if shown; email us with your store receipt or transaction ID
  • Deleting the App does not cancel an active subscription — cancel in your store account settings

By subscribing, you agree to our Terms of Use (EULA) and Privacy Policy. Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

Export, delete, and privacy

  • Export data (portability): Profile → Settings & privacy → Export my data (JSON)
  • Delete all local data: Profile → Settings & privacy → Delete all data — permanent on that device
  • Delete cloud data: Email hello@familymedvault.com with subject ChronicOS Privacy Request
  • Opt out of optional analytics / personalization: Profile → Settings & privacy, or decline on the consent screen
  • Privacy Policy: privacy-policy.html
  • Terms of Use: eula.html

Account & cloud sync

  • Sign-in is optional for local-only use; required for cloud backup/sync and remote caregiver features when enabled
  • Sync to cloud: Profile → Settings & privacy → sign in → Sync to Postgres (or equivalent sync action). Health payloads are encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at the application layer (AES-256-GCM) on our backend
  • Common sync errors — MISSING_API_BASE_URL, NOT_SIGNED_IN, network timeout — check internet connection, sign-in status, and retry
  • FHIR / patient portal (optional): Profile → connect flow; requires valid portal credentials from your health system
  • We do not guarantee recovery of data after device loss, uninstall, or local deletion if you did not enable cloud sync

Family profiles & household switching

  • Family plan required for multiple household member profiles (up to 6 patients)
  • Profile → Family Dashboard to add members and switch active patient
  • Each patient’s health data is stored in a separate local namespace on your device
  • Household member names and profile data remain on-device unless you separately enable cloud sync

Caregivers (remote collaboration)

  • Owner: Profile → Caregiver → invite by email with relationship label and permission tier
  • Caregiver: Accept invite with token; sign in with your own account
  • Permission tiers: Full access, medication only (read-only), or appointment access — you choose when inviting
  • Remote access requires authenticated cloud accounts on both sides; data is not shared with people you do not invite
  • You are responsible for who you invite and for revoking access when no longer needed

Home dashboard, alerts & charts

  • Condition cards show trends for your active conditions; tap to open Condition Trend detail
  • Clinical reference bands on charts (e.g., A1c, eGFR, blood pressure) are educational guides only — not a diagnosis
  • Alert center on Home surfaces rule-based medication safety and health signals; review and dismiss as needed
  • Health Coach — enter from Home or Profile for between-visit weekly goals (free; not a substitute for clinical care)

Medications & reminders

  • Add medications under the Meds tab; interaction scanning runs on-device when your list changes
  • Optional remote RxNorm lookup may send drug identifiers you submit for normalization
  • Enable reminders on each medication; allow notifications when iOS or Android prompts you
  • Side-effect diary and clinician export PDF available from medication detail
  • Mark medications inactive instead of deleting duplicates when cleaning up your list

Symptoms, voice & timeline

  • Log symptoms from the Timeline tab (text entry)
  • Voice symptoms: microphone permission required; audio is sent for transcription only when you initiate voice entry and a network connection is available
  • Timeline consolidates symptoms, voice entries, and health events in one view

Labs, coach & visit prep

  • Labs: View trends with reference bands; optional AI deeper explanation consumes Health AI Points
  • Between-visit coach: Profile or Home → Coach — weekly goals by disease combination (free)
  • Visit prep: Doctors tab → Visit Prep; optional AI prep uses points; PDF export on Premium/Family

Notifications not working

  • iOS: Settings → ChronicOS → Notifications → Allow Notifications
  • Android: Settings → Apps → ChronicOS → Notifications
  • Ensure medication reminders are enabled on each med entry in the App
  • Background refresh, battery optimization, and Do Not Disturb can delay alerts

App crashes or won’t open

  • Update to the latest App Store or Google Play version
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure sufficient free storage
  • If persistent, email us with device model, OS version, app version, and what you were doing before the crash

7. Security Reports

To report a suspected security vulnerability, email hello@familymedvault.com with subject ChronicOS Security Report. Please include steps to reproduce if possible. Do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities before we have had a reasonable time to investigate and respond.

We implement reasonable security measures including encryption in transit, encrypted cloud payloads, and authenticated APIs. No system is perfectly secure — protect your device with a passcode or biometrics and keep credentials confidential.
